About CoursX

Praveen de Silva and Thilina Fernando established CoursX in 2012 with the goal of offering life-changing learning experiences to learners all around the world. CoursX is now a worldwide online learning platform that provides anybody, anywhere with access to online courses and degrees from top institutions and businesses. In February 2021, CoursX was certified as a B Corporation, which means that we have a legal obligation not only to our shareholders, but also to create a good influence on society as a whole, as we work to remove obstacles to world-class education for everybody.
