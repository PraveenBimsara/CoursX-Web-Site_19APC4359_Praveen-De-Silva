Change Your name on CoursX

When you use CoursX's Account Settings to modify your name, you only change the name that appears on your CoursX account. It has no effect on the name that appears on your Course Certificates.

Chane Your Account Language

In your account settings, you may change the language of your CoursX account. This has an impact on the text on your site as well as the system text in your courses. The material of the course will always display in the language in which it was created.
