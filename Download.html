Downloading Videos

You can download video files to watch them offline if you don't have access to the internet. You may also read the subtitles from the video as a transcript file. To watch downloaded movies on your computer, you'll need a video player. One decent choice is VLC Media Player.

Download Videos on a Computer

To download videos on a computer :

1. Open the video you want to download.

2. Under the video, find the Downloads section.

3. Click Lecture Video.

4. Once the video is downloaded to your computer, you can open it using the your video program of your computer.
