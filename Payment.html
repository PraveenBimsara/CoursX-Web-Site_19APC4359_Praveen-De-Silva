Payments on CoursX

On CoursX, some courses are available for a one-time fee that lasts for 180 days. On the course home page, you can see how much a course costs. A sales tax may be included in some payments in some places. The amount of sales tax will be shown on the checkout page.

Save Your Payment Information

When you submit your credit card information in CoursX, it will be recorded so that you may use it again in the future. When you sign up for a subscription or a free trial, your credit card will be charged every month unless you cancel it.

Payment Policies

You can pay for a course at any point if you begin taking it for free. Any assignments you submitted before paying for a course will credit toward your Certificate once you have paid for it.
