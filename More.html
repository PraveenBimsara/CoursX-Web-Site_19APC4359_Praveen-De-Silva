Read about CoursX

Praveen de Silva and Thilina Fernando, two university Students, founded CoursX in 2012 with the goal of providing global access to world-class learning. With over million enrolled learners as of September 30, 2021, it is currently one of the world's largest online learning platforms. CoursX a collaborates with most of  universities and businesses to provide a diverse range of material and credentials, including Guided Projects, courses, Specializations, certifications, and bachelor's and master's degrees. CoursX is used by institutions all around the world to upskill and reskill their workers, citizens, and students in a variety of high-demand sectors, such as data science, technology, and business.
